---
title: Uncle Harry Cookies
---
Exact amounts can be adjusted according to the size box/bag of cereal or marshmallows you get. The important thing is equal parts marshmallow and cereal with 1 Tb of butter per 2 Oz of marshmallow. You can add Peanut butter to your preference. The amounts listed here will fill a 9x13 casserole.

## Ingredients

* 5 Tb Salted Butter
* 1 C Chunky Peanut Butter
* 10 Oz bag of mini marshmallows
* 10 Oz bag of Cocoa Krispies

## Instructions

* Combine marshmallows and butter in a bowl. 
* Microwave for 1 minute
* Mix in Peanut Butter. 
* Microwave for 1 minute.
* Fold in Cereal
* Spray a 9x13 casserole with cooking spray.
* Put your cookie mixture in the casserole dish and pat down. 
* Allow to rest until firm before cutting and serving.
