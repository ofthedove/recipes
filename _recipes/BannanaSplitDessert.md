title: Banana Split Dessert

Passed down for generations

## Ingredients
### Crust
- 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
- 6 tbsp. oleo

### Filling
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 eggs
- 1 stick oleo

### Topping
- 3-4 bananas
- 1 can crushed pineapple (drained)
- 1 qt cool whip (or any whipped cream)
- 1/2 cup nuts
- 1/2 cup to 1 cup maraschino cherries

## Instructions
1. Mix together the graham cracker crumbs and 6 tbsp oleo and pack in the bottom of a 9x13 pan.
2. Blend until fluffy the powdered sugar, vanilla, eggs, and stick of oleo. Spread over crumbs
3. Slice bananas lengthwise and place over the filling, then spread the crushed pineapple over the top.
4. Top with cool whip
5. Sprinkle nuts and cherries on top
6. Refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight

## Notes
Oleo is an older shortening of the name "oleomargerine", which is now commonly called margerine. When we make the recipe, we use butter.

This recipe contains uncooked eggs. For food safety reasons, pasteurized eggs should be used.
