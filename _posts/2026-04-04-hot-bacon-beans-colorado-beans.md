## Ingredients
- 8 slices Bacon (approx 1/2 lb)
- 4 medium Onions
- 1/2 cup Brown sugar
- 1/2 cup Vinegar
- 1 (No. 303) can Green Limas
- 1 (No. 303) can Dried Limas
- 1 (No. 303) can Pork & Beans
- 1 (No. 303) can Kidney Beans

## Instructions
- Fry bacon crisp, chop, and fry onions in bacon grease until transparent.
- Add sugar and vinegar.
- Drain all beans except the Pork & Beans, add to onion mixture.
- Bake for 1 hour in a 325°F oven.
