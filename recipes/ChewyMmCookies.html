Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 12 tablespoons unsalted butter melted
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 yolk
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups M&Ms

Instructions

  • Heat oven to 425Â°F. If you are planning on making cookies that are not as large as the 1/4 cup called for here, bake at 375. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  • In a medium-sized bowl, whisk the flour, salt, and baking soda.
  • In a large mixing bowl, whisk the melted butter and the sugars until well combined. Whisk in the egg, yolk and vanilla until well combined. With a flexible spatula, gently fold in the dry ingredients until just combined.
  • Scoop 1/4 cup balls of dough using an ice cream scoop or dry measuring cup and space the dough balls about 2 in apart on the prepared sheets.
  • Bake the cookies, one sheet at a time for about 8 to 10 minutes. Check around half way, if they are browning more quickly than youâ€™d like reduce the temp to 375. Cookies might seem a little light in color at this point.
  • Let cool about 5 min before transferring to wire racks or towels, cool completely before storing.
